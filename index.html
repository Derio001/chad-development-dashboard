About This Dashboard

The Chad Development Intelligence Dashboard is built using official government statistical data from INSEED (Institut National de la Statistique, des Études Économiques et Démographiques) and the Chad Data Portal (chad.opendataforafrica.org).

This dashboard integrates 12 official datasets covering health, nutrition, GDP, food security, employment, and demography — making Chad's development data accessible and actionable for NGOs, development organizations, and policymakers.
